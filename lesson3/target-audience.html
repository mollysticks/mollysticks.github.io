Target Audience:

Our target audience will be those residents living in Austin, TX and surrounding areas.

Personas:

Mother of young kids

  • Name: Jane Smith
  • Occupation: Stay at Home Parent
  • Mother of two
  • Married, 34 years old
  • Has a bachelors degree in Biology
  • Goal: tries to offer as many diverse learning experiences for her children as she can, including outdoor activities.
  • She is an expert in surfing the web on a mobile device, so she will access it through there.

Young college student

  • Name: John Pace
  • Occupation: Student
  • Studying communications at the University of Texas at Austin
  • Single
  • He has a firm grasp on computers and uses the internet daily
  • He looks up the weather before leaving every day in order to be prepared to walk to all of his classes, often outside, between buildings

Scenarios

  • John has to walk outside the most on Mondays, where the distance between classes is greatest. He looks up the weather for that day so he can be prepared with an umbrella or jacket.
  • Jane wants to take her kids to the park, but not if it is raining, so she checks the site.
  • Jane wants to wash her car, but not if it is about to rain, so she checks.
  • John generally parks on the road near his aparment because it's free, but hears about a storm coming. He checks the website for the possibility of hail to see if he needs to move his car to a garage.
  • John wants to play capture the flag at midnight with his local YSA group on campus, but not if it is going to be bad weather. He checks the weather the day of to make sure it will work out.
