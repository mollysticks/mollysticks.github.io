A Winter Storm is Coming Again!

In what's become a common theme lately, multiple winter weather advisories have been issued for East Idaho because of yet another snowstorm expected to barrel through the region.

The storm is forecast to bring snow to East Idaho Sunday night through Monday night and follows multiple winter storms that passed through the region last week.

The weather service is urging motorists to slow down and use caution while driving in East Idaho during the winter weather. The Monday morning commute could be especially hazardous because of the storm, the weather service said.

Be careful out there in the storms that are going to continue to come and stay tuned for further updates.
